Quick Checklists

Basic Emergency Kit

  • Water (1 gallon per person per day)
  • Non-perishable food
  • First aid kit
  • Flashlight and extra batteries
  • Whistle
  • Dust masks
  • Moist towelettes and garbage bags
  • Wrench or pliers
  • Manual can opener
  • Local maps
  • Cell phone with chargers

Important Documents

  • Identification documents
  • Insurance policies
  • Bank account records
  • Medical records
  • Emergency contact list
  • Family photos
  • Property records

Contact Information

For questions about ward emergency preparedness, please contact your ward emergency preparedness specialist or reach out to the bishopric.

Important Numbers

  • Emergency: 911
  • Non-Emergency Police: Contact your local department
  • Poison Control: 1-800-222-1222
